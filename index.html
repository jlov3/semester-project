Pig

A Dice Game

How to play Pig

  • There is one dice.
  • There are two players (you, as the user, and the computer).
  • Each round consists of one turn for each player. You go first.
  • On your first move of a turn, you must roll the die.
  • After your first roll, you may choose to roll or to hold.
  • Rolls will add together for points you earn that turn. You have up to 10 rolls per turn.
  • Holding will end your turn, and add the points you earned this round to your total score.
  • However, if you roll a ONE , your turn will immediately end and no points will be added to your score.
  • The computer will play its turn, and then the round will end
  • The game continues until a player has reached 100 or more points.
